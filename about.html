What we're up to

At Hyperfine, we're currently exploring the fascinating world of sour beer. From sophisticated and mature barrel aged sours, to sour interpretations of popular new styles like the New England IPA, we are leaving no stone unturned. Partnering with yeast labs and fermentation experts, we are expanding our understanding of the microbiology behind sour beer production, and applying our knowledge to create superlative products.

In the works

  • Sour IPA Series
    Sour renditions of the New England IPA style. Fruity, hop forward, tart and refreshing, these beers epitomize “juiciness”.
  • Golden Sour
    Aged for 12-18 months in oak barrels, aged with fruit, herbs, or spices, and often blended into our other products to add a zip of acidity or tropical fruit flavor, this beer is the cornerstone of our brewing program.
  • Sour Stout
    Silky chocolate, smooth vanilla, and rich coffee flavors combine with a mild acidity to produce what we best describe as chocolate covered fruit. A beer designed for special occasions and long term cellaring.
  • Sour Red
    A modern rendition of a classic style. A Flander’s red base, aged in rum barrels, and blended with locally grown stone fruits and berries creates a complex and layered beer. It will take you on a flavor journey through lemony acidity, ripe cherry, deep leather and tobacco, and spiced rum.
  • Berliner Weisse with Local Yeast
    Featuring our house yeast strain isolated from an oak tree in Fresh Pond, It produces light clove, jasmine, and banana flavors - enhancing the tart, highly effervescent, and crisp qualities of the beer.

Our mission

HYPERFINE is a mission driven brewery, committed to innovation in brewing science, sustainable manufacturing and community engagement. We take an experimental and iterative approach to brewing - creating unique and inspired products that embody our values and reflect our commitments.
